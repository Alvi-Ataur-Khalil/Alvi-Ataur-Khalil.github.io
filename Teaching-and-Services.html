Digital Forensics - Undergraduate and Graduate Levels (CS413 & CS513)

- Study of different legal and ethical issues, storage system, file system, forensics tools.

- Hands-on experience on network, cloud and database forensics, admissibility of digital evidence, and counter-forensics reporting.
