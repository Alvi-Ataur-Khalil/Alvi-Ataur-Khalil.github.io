Multiple Fully Funded Ph.D. Positions Available

I am seeking highly motivated and hard-working Ph.D. students to join my team, beginning in Spring 2026. The students will work in the broad areas of cybersecurity, AI-enabled autonomy, and blockchain security.

 ​Basic Eligibility

  • B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science, Electrical/Computer Engineering, or a closely related field
  • Solid foundation in programming, algorithms, and logic
  • Demonstrated problem-solving and critical-thinking skills
  • Meets SIUC graduate‐admission standards

 ​Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior research or industry experience in cybersecurity, CPS/IoT, AI/ML, or blockchain
  • Scholarly publications (conference or journal)
  • Strong software engineering practices (version control, testing, reproducible workflows)
  • GRE scores: Quant ≥ 160

 ​How to Apply

Email Me at a.khalil@siu.edu with all of the following (subject line: “Prospective PhD Student [Spring 2026]”):

  1. Curriculum Vitae
  2. Unofficial transcripts (undergraduate & graduate, if applicable)
  3. Brief statement of research interest (≤ 1 page)
  4. GRE scores
  5. English proficiency scores (IELTS/TOEFL)
